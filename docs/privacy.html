Privacy Policy — Ezwipe

Effective date: 2026-03-17

Summary

Ezwipe helps you review and clean up photos stored on your device. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What data we collect

  • Photos and media: Ezwipe accesses photos and media on your device only with your permission. Photos remain stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers by default.
  • Analytics & diagnostics: We collect anonymous usage and crash reports to improve the app (e.g., via Firebase Analytics and Sentry). These reports may include device model, OS version, and non-identifying usage events.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to show ads. Ad providers may collect device identifiers and other information according to their policies.
  • Purchase information: For in-app purchases we use platform billing (Apple/Google). Purchase receipts and product identifiers are handled by the stores; we do not collect payment card details.
  • Optional content: If you contact support you may provide email or other identifying information which we will store to respond.

How we use your data

  • Provide and improve app features (local photo grouping, cleanup flows).
  • Deliver analytics and crash reports to diagnose issues and prioritize fixes.
  • Serve ads and respect consent choices for personalized ads.
  • Process in-app purchases (restore, entitlement checks) via platform APIs.

Third-party services

We use third-party services that have their own privacy policies. Major services used include:

  • Google Play / Apple App Store (billing, receipts)
  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Firebase (analytics)
  • Sentry (crash reporting)

Data retention

Analytics and crash logs are retained by the respective providers according to their policies. Local app data (preferences, cached hashes) is kept on device unless you delete the app or clear app data.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored by the app. Note that photos and media are stored on your device and are subject to the device's security protections.

Your choices

  • You can revoke photo access in your device Settings at any time; doing so will limit Ezwipe functionality.
  • To opt out of analytics or reset ad identifiers, follow the platform settings or use the app's Ad Preferences (Settings → Manage Ad Preferences).
  • To request deletion of support-provided personal data, contact us at privacy@ezwipe.app.

Children

Ezwipe is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). If you believe we have collected personal information about a child, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: privacy@ezwipe.app.
